Scam Alerts

  • Fake Loan App: Targets students with false promises.
  • Phishing SMS: Pretends to be Safaricom, asks for PIN.
  • WhatsApp Chain Scam: Promises free data bundles.

Safe Phone Setup

  1. Install apps only from trusted sources.
  2. Use a strong screen lock and PIN.
  3. Disable location access for unnecessary apps.
  4. Review app permissions regularly.
  5. Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks.

General Safety Tips

Always update your software, avoid clicking unknown links, and back up your data regularly.
